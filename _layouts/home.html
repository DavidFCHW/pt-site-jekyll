Pilgrim Tabernacle

"Wherefore Jesus also, that he might sanctify the people with His own blood, suffered without the gate. Let us go forth therefore unto Him without the camp, bearing His reproach. For here have we no continuing city, but we seek one to come." - Hebrews 13:12-14

Welcome!

Welcome to Pilgrim Tabernacle! We are sojourners in this world seeking the heavenly kingdom to come in Jesus Christ. As a body of believers, we exist to glorify God through the gospel and hope that the resources on this site will help you believe in the Lord Jesus Christ and to continue believing in Him for eternal life.


Our Services

Please join and fellowship with us at the following meetings:

Sunday Services

Worship with us at our Lord's Day services, as we listen to the preaching of God's word, sing hymns and share communion.

Services at 10:30am and 6:00pm.

Bible Studies

Join us for in depth teaching, study and discussion of God's Word. Refreshments will also be provided!

Bible Studies on Tuesdays at 7:00pm.

Prayer Meetings

Come and seek the Lord with us at our prayer meetings as we intercede for one another and for God's people.

Prayer Meetings on Thursdays at 7:00pm.
